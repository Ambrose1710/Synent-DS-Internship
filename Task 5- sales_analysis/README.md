# Task 5 - Sales Data Analysis

## Problem Statement
Analyze Superstore sales data to understand business performance through revenue trends, top-selling products and profit analysis.

## 📊 Dataset
- **Source:** [Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)
- **File:** superstore.csv
- **Size:** 9,994 orders, 21 columns

## 🔑 Key Insights
1. **Monthly Revenue Trend:** Revenue shows overall growth with recurring seasonal spikes. Notably, revenue troughs never fall below previous lows - indicating a steadily rising baseline and healthy growth.

2. **Profit by Category:** Technology is the most profitable category (~$140,000), 
followed by Office Supplies (~$120,000). Furniture significantly underperforms 
(~$20,000), possibly due to pricing strategy or product quality issues

3. **Profit by Region:** The West region leads in profitability (~$100,000), followed 
by the East (~$90,000). Central and South regions lag significantly, warranting 
further investigation into regional market conditions
